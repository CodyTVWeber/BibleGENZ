# Psalm Chapter 148

    1. Praise Yah! Praise Yahweh from the heavens! Praise him in the heights!
    2. Praise him, all his angels! Praise him, all his army!
    3. Praise him, sun and moon! Praise him, all ya shining stars!
    4. Praise him, ya heavens of heavens, ya waters that are above the heavens.
    5. Let them praise the name of Yahweh, For he commanded, and they were created.
    6. He has also established them forever and ever. He has made a decree which won't pass away.
    7. Praise Yahweh from the earth, ya major sea creatures, and all depths!
    8. Lightning and hail, snow and clouds; stormy wind, fulfilling his word;
    9. mountains and all hills; fruit trees and all cedars;
  10. wild animals and all livestock; tiny creatures and flying birds;
  11. kings of the earth and all peoples; princes and all judges of the earth;
  12. both young men and maidens; old men and children:
  13. let them praise the name of Yahweh, for his name alone is exalted. His glory is above the earth and the heavens.
  14. He has lifted up the horn of his people, the praise of all his yeeted fam; even of the children of Israel, a people near to him. Praise Yah!
