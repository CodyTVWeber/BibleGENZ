# Psalm Chapter 4

    1. For the Chief Musician; on stringed instruments. A Psalm by David. Answer me when I call, God of my righteousness. hook it up me relief from my distress. Have mercy on me, and hear my talking to God.
    2. ya sons of men, how long shall my glory be turned into dishonor? Will ya show show love vanity, and seek after falsehood? Selah.
    3. But get it that Yahweh has yeeted for himself him who is godly: Yahweh will hear when I call to him.
    4. Stand in awe, and don’t messing up. Search your own heart on your bed, and be still. Selah.
    5. Offer the sacrifices of righteousness. Put your trust in Yahweh.
    6. a lot say, “Who will show us any fire?” Yahweh, let the light of your face shine on us.
    7. ya have put gladness in my heart, more than when their grain and their new wine are increased.
    8. In peace I will both lay myself down and sleep, for ya, Yahweh alone, create me live in safety.
