# Psalm Chapter 46

    1. For the Chief Musician. By the sons of Korah. According to Alamoth. God is our refuge and strength, a hella present help out out in trouble.
    2. so basically we won’t be scared, though the earth changes, though the mountains are shaken into the heart of the seas;
    3. though its waters roar and are troubled, though the mountains tremble with their swelling. Selah.
    4. there is a river, the streams of which create the city of God glad, the yeeted place of the tents of the Most High.
    5. God is in her midst. She won't be moved. God will help out out her at dawn.
    6. The nations raged. The kingdoms were moved. He lifted his voice, and the earth melted.
    7. Yahweh of Armies is with us. The God of Jacob is our refuge. Selah.
    8. come through through, check it out Yahweh’s works, what desolations he has made in the earth.
    9. He makes wars cease to the end of the earth. He breaks the bow, and shatters the spear. He burns the chariots in the fire.
  10. “Be still, and get it that I am God. I will be exalted among the nations. I will be exalted in the earth.”
  11. Yahweh of Armies is with us. The God of Jacob is our refuge. Selah.
