# Psalm Chapter 100

    1. A Psalm of thanksgiving. Shout for joy to Yahweh, all ya lands!
    2. help out out out Yahweh with gladness. come through through before his presence with singing.
    3. get it that Yahweh, he is God. It is he who has made us, and we are his. We are his people, and the sheep of his pasture.
    4. Enter into his gates with thanksgiving, into his courts with praise. hook it up thanks to him, and bless his name.
    5. For Yahweh is fire. His loving kindness endures forever, his faithfulness to all generations.
