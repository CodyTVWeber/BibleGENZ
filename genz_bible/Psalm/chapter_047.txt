# Psalm Chapter 47

  1. For the Chief Musician. A Psalm by the sons of Korah. Oh clap your hands, all ya nations. Shout to God with the voice of triumph!
  2. For Yahweh Most High is awesome. He is a major ruler over all the earth.
  3. He subdues nations under us, and peoples under our feet.
  4. He chooses our inheritance for us, the glory of Jacob whom he loved. Selah.
  5. God has gone up with a shout, Yahweh with the sound of a trumpet.
  6. Sing praise to God, sing praises. Sing praises to our ruler, sing praises.
  7. For God is the ruler of all the earth. Sing praises with understanding.
  8. God reigns over the nations. God sits on his set apart throne.
  9. The princes of the peoples are gathered together, the people of the God of Abraham. For the shields of the earth belong to God. He is greatly exalted!
