# Psalm Chapter 29

  1. A Psalm by David. Ascribe to Yahweh, you sons of the mighty, ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength.
  2. Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to his name. giving props Yahweh in set apart array.
  3. Yahweh’s voice is on the waters. The God of glory thunders, even Yahweh on a lot waters.
  4. Yahweh’s voice is powerful. Yahweh’s voice is full of majesty.
  5. The voice of Yahweh breaks the cedars. Yes, Yahweh breaks in pieces the cedars of Lebanon.
  6. He makes them also to skip like a calf; Lebanon and Sirion like a young, wild ox.
  7. Yahweh’s voice strikes with flashes of lightning.
  8. Yahweh’s voice shakes the wilderness. Yahweh shakes the wilderness of Kadesh.
  9. Yahweh’s voice makes the deer calve, and strips the forests bare. In his God's house everything says, “Glory!”
 10. Yahweh sat enthroned at the Flood. Yes, Yahweh sits as ruler forever.
 11. Yahweh will hook it up strength to his people. Yahweh will bless his people with peace.
